Made for early Dutch learners who read online.

DutchMate is intentionally small: it focuses on the moments where you understand most of a page, but need a quick bit of help to continue.

Works on normal webpages

Use it while reading Dutch news, blogs, forms, and learning material in the browser.

Useful multilingual support

The MVP focuses on Dutch, English, and Telugu, matching the first learner audience.

Small, focused controls

Turn hover and selection translation on or off, change languages, and clear the local cache.

Clear about what gets sent.

DutchMate has to send the text you ask to translate to its backend, which uses Google Cloud Translation. It does not require an account for the MVP and does not sell user data.

You choose the text

Hovered words and selected text may be sent for translation depending on your settings and actions.

Local cache stays local

Successful selected single-word lookups can be cached in your browser to make repeated lookups faster.

Private pages need care

Avoid using DutchMate on sensitive pages if you do not want that text sent for translation.
