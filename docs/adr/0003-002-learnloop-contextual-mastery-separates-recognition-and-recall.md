# Contextual mastery separates recognition and recall

DutchMate will track recognition and recall mastery separately, with overall mastery limited by the weaker dimension. Practice will remain a consistent tap-to-reveal flashcard interaction with no typed input; an Again or Got it result will update the dimension tested by the card direction and inform adaptive spacing. This accepts less objective evidence than typed testing in exchange for a substantially simpler daily experience, while avoiding the misleading claim that recognizing Dutch and recalling Dutch are the same skill.
