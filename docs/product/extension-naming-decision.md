# Extension Naming Decision

Reviewed: 2026-06-15

## Decision Status

Status: **Current direction**

Decision: **Keep DutchMate for the Dutch-focused MVP.**

Possible future expansion brand: **LingoMaat**.

## Context

The extension is currently named DutchMate. The first product focus is Dutch because:

- the creator lives in the Netherlands,
- the creator is actively learning Dutch,
- there is direct access to a WhatsApp community of Dutch learners,
- early users are most likely to care about a tool that clearly helps with Dutch.

This makes a Dutch-specific name strategically useful for the first audience.

## Names Explored

Names that were liked but appeared to already be taken online for existing products, websites, or apps:

- LightLingo
- LingoLight
- DutchSpark
- DutchLingo
- LingoDutch

Names that seemed more available or worth keeping in consideration:

- DutchMate
- LingoMaat
- LumiLingo

Other explored directions included names around "light", "lingo", "maat", "Dutch", and "buddy/companion" positioning.

## Naming Rationale

DutchMate is the strongest name for the current stage because it is immediately clear to the likely first users:

```text
This helps me with Dutch.
```

That clarity matters for a browser extension because potential users are scanning quickly and deciding whether the tool is relevant and trustworthy.

LingoMaat is broader and more expandable. It combines:

- `Lingo`: language learning, not limited to Dutch.
- `Maat`: Dutch for mate/buddy, preserving some Dutch character.

However, for the first Dutch-learning audience, LingoMaat is less instantly specific than DutchMate. It may require extra explanation before users understand that the extension helps with Dutch.

LumiLingo is broader and polished, but it feels less connected to the creator's current story and less specific to the first user group.

## Critical Tradeoff

DutchMate has one clear downside: it limits the perceived scope to Dutch.

That is acceptable for now because the product has not yet earned the complexity of a multi-language platform brand. The better early bet is to optimize for the clearest current wedge:

```text
Dutch learners in and around the Netherlands.
```

If DutchMate gets meaningful traction, expansion becomes a good problem to have.

## Future Expansion Path

If the product later expands beyond Dutch, possible paths include:

- keep DutchMate as the Dutch product,
- introduce LingoMaat as the parent brand,
- transition to "DutchMate by LingoMaat",
- launch future products such as "LingoMaat German" or "LingoMaat Spanish",
- rename the broader product after there is enough momentum to justify it.

The reserved future architecture can be:

```text
LingoMaat
-> DutchMate
-> future language products
```

## Current Positioning

Recommended current positioning:

```text
DutchMate: your Dutch learning companion for the web.
```

This keeps the message simple, direct, and aligned with the first audience.
